Chaldron Protocol

Seamlessly move tokens from one chain to another

The Chaldron protocol creates a decentralized bridge for tokens between blockchains, initially operating on EVM chains with plans to expand to other networks. The protocol uses a simple burn-and-mint mechanism that eliminates the need for liquidity pools, third-party services, or complex lock mechanisms.
